Welcome to my little space in the World Wide Web

Hello, I am Lissa!

I am a twenty-something year old Brazilian-German cheese enthusiast, currently living in Austria. I love creating. For me being creative means expressing myself in different ways, wether it be through drawing, taking photographs, writing or randomly making my own jewellery.

As I grew up bilingually with German and Portuguese, the concepts of language and different cultures have always been a very present aspect in my life. I find it fascinating how our language and culture can influence the way we perceive the world. Language is such a powerful tool that helps us to tell impactful stories. Having always been a little bookworm, writing has come naturally to me. That is why I decided to study transcultural communication at the University of Vienna, as it combines culture, language, writing and communication science.

My greatest passion, though, is photography and picture-editing. Beautiful aesthetics really please me, so if you asked me what my hobby was, I would tell you it is capturing random moments and details but also moods into one little frame and then edit them until I`m happy with the result.

Another great obsession of mine, besides chocolate of course, is music. Unfortunately, the gods of music did not bless me with any musical talent - nevertheless, I am still the happiest when listening and dancing to some good tunes or going to a nice concert with my friends.
